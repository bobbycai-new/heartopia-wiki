About Us

A Chinese-first unofficial Heartopia wiki & database

🎯 Our Mission

We provide practical, accurate Heartopia guides and data — with a Chinese-first focus, since most existing wikis overlook Chinese-speaking players.

Data is versioned: numeric fields come from public community datasets, while translations are continuously verified. Open, correctable data benefits everyone.
