Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

1. Overview

Heartopia Quest ("we", "this site", domain heartopia.quest) is an unofficial fan resource. This policy explains what we collect when you visit, how we use cookies, and how third-party advertising (including Google AdSense) works.

2. Information We Collect

This is a static website. We do not run user accounts or ask for personal information, and we do not run our own analytics or tracking.

  • Tool data (e.g. collection progress) is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and never uploaded.
  • Advertising and third-party services may collect information separately — see below.

3. Cookies & Google Advertising

To support this site, we display ads served by Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

Advertising cookies allow Google to tailor ads to your interests based on visits to this and other sites, and to measure campaign performance. These cookies do not identify you personally.

See How Google uses cookies for advertising 。

4. Your Choices

You can control advertising cookies and personalization in several ways:

  • Opt out of personalized Google ads at Google Ads Settings。
  • Opt out of many third-party vendors at once via aboutads.info/choices。
  • Clear or block cookies in your browser (may affect some site features).

5. Third-Party Vendors

Aside from Google AdSense, if other third-party services (analytics, comments, etc.) are added in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly. Third parties' use of collected data is governed by their own privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us to have it removed.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision; significant changes will be highlighted.

8. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Reach us via the Contact page .
