Heartopia Cooking Guide: Leveling, Recipes & Best Profit

Cooking is Heartopia’s most profitable hobby — turn fishing/gardening raw materials into dishes for 2-3× gold.

📌 Mechanics & profit from community research. Recipe counts vary by source (~77-98); verify in-game.

1. Unlock

  • Reach DG Level 6.
  • Find Massimo at the town center restaurant, complete his tutorial.
  • Use the public stove or craft your own.

Recommended: at DG6 choose Cooking first — synergizes with Fishing & Gardening.

2. Mechanics

Flow

Interact with stove → pick recipe → add ingredients → QTE heat control → collect.

Watch the heat

Miss the QTE → “Bizarre Food/Drink” (sells for less) — focus!

Star quality

1-5 stars; higher star = higher sell price.

Multiple stoves

Craft extra stoves (timber + ore) for mass production.

4. Leveling

Cook to gain XP. Approx: ~100 (basic), ~250-500 (Lv2-3), ~850-1300 (Lv4-5), ~1900 (Lv6), ~2700-3700 (Lv7-8), 5000+ (Lv10+).

Use multiple stoves; cook quick recipes early (more XP/hour).

5. High-Profit Recipes

Starter (auto-learned)

  • House Salad = Any Vegetable ×2
  • Mixed Jam = Mixed Fruits ×4

Lv1 (buy from Massimo)

  • Fish and Chips = Any Fish ×2 + Potato ×2
  • Cheesecake = Cheese + Milk + Wheat
  • Grilled Mushroom = Any Mushroom ×4

6. Tips

  1. Cooking first — synergizes with Fishing/Gardening.
  2. Multiple stoves for mass production.
  3. Focus high-profit: Strawberry Jam, Grape Jam, Latte.
  4. Don’t burn — QTE focus.
  5. Push high star.
  6. Free fruit → jam = zero-cost profit.
