Heartopia Flower Crossbreeding: 1★ to 5★

Crossbreeding combines two flowers to create rarer, higher-star varieties.

📌 Mechanics & colors from community research. Growth time & success rate vary by source; verify in-game.

1. Unlock

  • Unlock Gardening hobby (~DG Level 3).
  • Buy 1-star seeds (Red/Yellow/White) from the Flower Shop.

2. Star Tier System

3. Mechanics

Adjacent planting

Two parents in adjacent tiles (horizontal/vertical/diagonal), same tier or within 1 tier.

Growth cycle

Parents bloom → hybrid seed grows in adjacent empty tile.

Growth time varies by source (18h vs 24h) — verify in-game. Keep an empty tile!

Watering

Water parents daily. Friend watering boosts success rate.

Collect

Seed icon appears → walk through bush to collect.

5. Success Rate

Friend watering (key)

  • Self only: low
  • +1 friend: ~+10%
  • +2 friends: ~+30%
  • +3 friends: optimal (~60-70%)

Find watering partners on the official Discord #gardening.

Rainbow Breeding Powder

  • 100% success on one pair of bloomed flowers.
  • Source: events, shop (~350 Moonlight Crystals).
  • Won’t work on 5★ (e.g. Selene Daisy).

6. Tips

  1. Checkerboard layout (leave empty tiles).
  2. Max ~12 plants active.
  3. Water daily.
  4. Keep empty tiles adjacent to parents.
  5. Save rainbow powder for rare combos.
