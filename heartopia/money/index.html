Heartopia Money Guide: Fastest Gold Route

Full money route: golden rule, trinity (Cooking/Gardening/Fishing), phase strategies & daily loop.

📌 Based on community research + site recipe/fish data. Verify values in-game.

1. Golden Rule: Never Sell Raw

Raw fish/fruit/veg sell cheap. Process into dishes for 2-3× profit.

A raw apple sells for pennies; an apple pie/jam sells for hundreds.

2. Where to Sell: Albert Jr.

No shipping bin — sell to the gold merchant Albert Jr.:

  • Wanders town (track his icon on map).
  • “Sell Items” → batch sell.
  • Liquidate cooked food + rare fish in one trip.

3. Trinity of Wealth

🍳 Cooking (fastest, most reliable)

  • Early zero-cost: shake trees/bushes for free fruit → jam = pure profit.
  • Cook low-star crops (QTE can upgrade stars).
  • Check Recipe Calculator for top profit.

🌱 Gardening (ingredient engine)

  • Early: Wheat + Potatoes (mid/late recipe staples).
  • Late: Grapes + Strawberries.
  • Time mgmt: fast crops while online; slow crops (Grapes >10h) before sleep.
  • Black Truffle: forage Forest Island (~15min respawn), legendary ingredient.

🎣 Fishing (jackpot)

  • Rain: Suburban Lake for Crucian Carp (fish-006) → cook to double value.
  • Rainbow: seas (Whale Sea / Calm Sea) for Rainbow Fish, huge sell price.
  • Don’t sell raw fish — cook it!

4. Phase Routes

Early (DG 1-6)

  1. Free fruit → jam → sell.
  2. Fish (rainy Carp) → cook.
  3. Plant Wheat + Potato.
  4. DG6 → unlock Cooking first.

Mid (DG 6-12)

  1. Level cooking, unlock high-profit recipes (Latte, Grape Jam).
  2. Multiple stoves.
  3. Target rare fish.
  4. Stock Grapes/Strawberries.

Late (DG 12+)

  1. Master recipes (Tiramisu, Supreme Cake).
  2. Rainbow Fish + legendary dishes.
  3. Black Truffle dishes.

5. Daily Money Loop

  1. Claim daily / codes
  2. Water + harvest
  3. Fish (weather priority)
  4. Forage (fruit/mushroom/truffle)
  5. Batch cook (multiple stoves)
  6. Sell to Albert Jr.

Use Daily Checklist.

6. One-liner

Cooking first · Don’t sell raw · Free fruit → jam · Rain/Rainbow fish · Sell to Albert Jr.
