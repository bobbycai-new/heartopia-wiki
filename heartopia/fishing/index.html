🎯 Fishing Tips

Best Times

  • Early Morning (6:00-9:00): Fish are most active
  • Evening (18:00-21:00): Higher rare fish spawn rate
  • Night (After 21:00): Legendary fish time window

Weather Impact

  • Sunny: Common fish active
  • Cloudy: Rare fish spawn rate ↑
  • Rainy: Rare fish spawn rate ↑↑
  • Stormy: Epic fish spawn rate ↑↑↑

Gear Selection

  • Basic Rod: Suitable for common fish
  • Advanced Rod: Increased catch rate
  • Legendary Rod: Required for legendary fish
  • Bait: Different baits attract different fish types

💎 Pro Tips

  1. Check Schedule: Each fish has specific spawn times
  2. Watch Weather: Rare fish appear in specific conditions
  3. Upgrade Gear: Better rods = higher success rate
  4. Use Right Bait: Target fish’s preferred bait
  5. Be Patient: Legendary fish require special conditions

🎣 Happy fishing! Remember to check for full moons and special weather events!
