Heartopia Pet Care: Cats & Dogs

Pets are a late-game hobby. Cat first, then dog — adopt, care, bond; dogs can learn commands.

📌 Mechanics from community research. Breed counts (~14 cats / ~11 dogs) & fees vary. Verify in-game.

1. Unlock (Late)

  • DG Level 12 (late unlock, multiple days).
  • Prereqs: Cooking, Gardening, Fishing, Birdwatching, Bug Catching unlocked.
  • Use Hobby Expansion Ticket on Cat Care.
  • Find Mrs. Joan (Adoption Center / Pet Shop) for tutorial.

⚠️ Must unlock Cat Care before Dog Care.

2. Adoption

  1. Go to Adoption Center (Mrs. Joan).
  2. Browse daily-rotating pets.
  3. Check breed/gender/personality.
  4. Pay adoption fee.
  5. Pet sent to your home.

Don’t like today’s? Come back tomorrow.

3. Personality

PersonalityTrait
EnergeticPlayful, needs more activity
ClingyWants attention, follows you
SensitiveGentle handling, startle-prone
SociableFriendly to all, great for visitors
IndifferentIndependent, harder to bond

4. Daily Care

  • 🍖 Feed: daily.
  • 🤚 Pet: bond.
  • 🛁 Bathe: clean.
  • 🎾 Play: especially Energetic.

5. Dog-Exclusive: Training

Dogs learn commands:

  • 🦴 Seek Treasure: dog finds treasure — very useful.
  • More unlock with training level. Walk + train daily for bond.

6. Tips

  1. Cat before dog — can’t skip.
  2. Check personality before adopting.
  3. Daily feed + pet for bond.
  4. Train Seek Treasure (dog).
  5. Wait for daily rotation if picky.
